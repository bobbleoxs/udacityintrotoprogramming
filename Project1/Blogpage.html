ANTHOLOGY OF DREAMS

The Desert Song — 2 June 2016

A lady fell down into sand dunes in a desert. Before she was completely swallowed by sand she gave her last kiss to the wannabe king — ‘when you succeed to be a king, sing this song that will wake me and bring me back to you’ then she fell silently into the sand that buried her completely. The man never succeeded in becoming a king and she was never brought back. The heart breaking pain woke me up.

The Valley of Flowers — years ago

I am walking in a valley of beautiful flowers with the man I adore.

‘What are these beautiful flowers?’ I asked.

The man replied, ‘They are the travellers came to the valley.’

‘Why are they staying here as flowers?’

‘Because they forgot their names.’ he turned around and looked into my eyes, ‘and your name is…?’

I forgot…
